About WatchSphere

WatchSphere is a free streaming index site that helps users discover popular Movies, TV Shows, and Anime from around the world. Our platform is powered by TMDB and third-party embeds to deliver trending content without needing registration or payment.

We aim to make entertainment accessible for everyone by providing a simple, fast, and responsive interface to explore what's trending across genres.

Note: WatchSphere does not host or upload any media files. All content is sourced from publicly available third-party platforms.
